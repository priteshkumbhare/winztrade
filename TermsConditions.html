Legality

Game of Skill are legal in India, as they are not governed by and excluded from the Public Gambling Act of 1867. The Indian Supreme Court has ruled that a game in which success depends mainly on the player’s intellectual knowledge, training, attention, experience, and adroitness is classified as a game of skill. This ruling was made in the cases of State of Andhra Pradesh v. K Satyanarayana and KR Lakshmanan v. State of Tamil Nadu. The competitions held on WinzTrade are purely game of skill as success/failure of Users depend primarily on their research and knowledge of concerned category and question. By registering and taking participating in the competition, the User agrees that he/she is participating in a game of skill.
